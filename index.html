New Community Structure

  • SURVambassadors control Group Hubs for Locations and Special Topics
  • BRANDambassadors control Hubs related to brands, dealers and vendors
  • Local Leaders are outreach admins for location based Hubs
  • Moderators have special permissions to flag, edit and or delete content and or members
  • Community members can contribute and edit content
